1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how YOUR_APP_NAME (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use the application.

YOUR_APP_NAME is a desktop application that connects to TikTok and YouTube using official OAuth flows to display analytics and statistics for your own accounts.

2. Information We Collect

The app only collects information that you explicitly authorize through TikTok and YouTube OAuth. Depending on the permissions you grant, this may include:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., display name, username, profile photo).
  • Account statistics (e.g., follower count, following count, likes count, video count).
  • Public video information (e.g., video titles, thumbnails, creation time, duration).
  • Video performance metrics (e.g., views, likes, comments, shares).

YOUR_APP_NAME does not collect your login password for TikTok or YouTube. Login is processed directly on the official platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information obtained via official APIs solely to:

  • Display profile and channel statistics inside the app.
  • Display analytics and performance metrics for your videos.
  • Help you compare and manage multiple accounts more easily.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Local Processing

YOUR_APP_NAME is designed as a local-first desktop application:

  • Access tokens and account data are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate a remote backend server that stores your data.
  • All analytics processing happens inside the app on your machine.

5. User Control and Revoking Access

You are in control of the permissions granted to YOUR_APP_NAME:

  • You can remove accounts from the app at any time, which will delete locally stored tokens and data for that account.
  • You may revoke API access from your TikTok and/or Google (YouTube) account settings, which invalidates the tokens held by the app.

6. Security

We apply standard security practices to protect the data stored locally on your device, but you are also responsible for the security of your computer, operating system, and local files.

7. Children’s Privacy

YOUR_APP_NAME is intended for use by individuals who are legally allowed to use TikTok and YouTube under their respective Terms of Service.
